A. How You Can Safeguard Your Privacy

While we're always working hard to keep your personal information safe, you must play an important role as well. Take care to prevent unauthorized persons from learning any of your Gamemine account information. You are solely responsible for safeguarding your e-mail received from Gamemine, username, and password. To prevent unauthorized use of your account, be sure to change your password often, and keep it in a safe place.

D. Why GameMine Collects Information

All of the personal information we collect is used to make Gamemine more enjoyable and more useful for our members.

When you join Gamemine, we ask you for certain personally identifying information, including your full name, e-mail address, postal address, telephone number, and billing information (such as a credit card number). We use this personally identifying information to create your Gamemine account, process your orders, and communicate with you about the status of your orders. We will use your phone number only for customer service-related calls, never for sales or marketing purposes. If you decide not to present personal information when prompted, you will limit the services we can give you.

E. Types of Information GameMine Collects

There are a number of kinds of information we accumulate from guests to our website.

They consist of:

  1. Information You Give Us

    When you first become a Gamemine member, we ask you to give us your name, address, phone number, credit card number, and other personally identifying information so that we can process and ship your orders. We may also use this information for such purposes as answering your requests, communicating order information to you, and improving our service.

  2. Automatic Information

    Like many websites, Gamemine.com sets "cookies" on your computer. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transmit to your computer's hard drive through your web browser, allowing us to automatically recognize you when you return to our site. They help us to match the games you've chosen to rent or buy with your individual account, and to personalize the Gamemine website for you. If cookies are not enabled on your web browser, you will not be able to use the Gamemine website or the Gamemine service. The "help" menu on most web browsers will tell you how to enable cookies.

  3. Order and Billing History

    Your order and billing history are also tracked automatically from the beginning of your Gamemine membership. This allows you to log-in to "My Account" and see the list of games, membership plans, or upgrades you've purchased from us.

F. Security

Gamemine uses a secure server to encrypt all of your personal information. The secure server employs 128-bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption, the most advanced encryption method available today. The SSL works for all information provided that results in a financial transaction either by membership or for purchase.

To protect your information from unauthorized access or use, we use only the most advanced technology and security features for the Gamemine website. We will continue to update and enhance our security practices as new technology becomes available. We have also implemented strict security guidelines for our employees, partners, and vendors.

While we make every effort to ensure the security of your personal information, no data transmission can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Therefore, please be aware that when you submit information to us, you do so at your own risk.

G. Sharing of Information

Except as described below, Gamemine does not sell or share your personally identifying information to third parties without:

  1. Notifying you of our intent to share such information.
  2. Giving you a chance to prevent your information from being shared.

We use outside companies, agents, and contractors to perform some important Gamemine tasks, such as processing credit card transactions and delivering your orders. In the course of doing so, these third parties can come into contact with some of your personally identifying information. However, we only give them access to the information they need in order to actually perform their tasks, and they are bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy - that is, they must treat your personal information the same way we do.

If we think that their products or services will interest our members, we may also partner with outside companies to send promotional announcements to some members. If you want to be excluded from these promotional mailings, you may opt out by logging in to "My Account" and updating your "E-mail Subscriptions".

We may, at our discretion, provide our prospective partners, advertisers, and other third parties with aggregate data about members and visitors to the Gamemine.com site. However, such data is anonymous, and we do not disclose personally identifying information about specific users.

Gamemine reserves the right to disclose users' personal information when suspected of fraud, intellectual property infringements, or further activity that is illegal requires us by law to do so.

In order to further provide additional offers and products, Gamemine will offer products from third parties by and through its own website, but it will not share your information with this third party. The offer will result in a call to action, for which the Gamemine user should take such action at their own discretion they can offer their personal information to the third party.

H. Updating Your Information

It is important to keep your information up to date as a subscription or purchasing customer to ensure proper delivery and maintain accurate billing information to avoid delay in shipment. If your shipping or billing information should change, you should immediately update your account information, which can be accessed by logging onto your Gamemine account from the link on the home page.

From that point, you must input your username and password and then update your account information from your Gamemine Control Panel. If for any reason you are unable to update your information or change any part of your information from the controls provided on "My Account", please contact Gamemine immediately for additional assistance. If you forget or misplace your password, Gamemine can additionally reset your password upon request and confirmation of identifying elements to your account.
